When someone visits the Foosball NFT Website, our web server automatically detects and logs some information about them. Email addresses are not stored on the site server. We solely use this data to enhance and better the content of the Foosball NFT website.

To monitor visitors on the Foosball NFT Website, we employ transient, session-specific cookies. Except as stated below, we do not sell, rent, or otherwise distribute the personal information of visitors to the Foosball NFT Website. We could divulge non-personal data to third parties who give us statistics about how the Foosball NFT Website is used.

We take reasonable security precautions to safeguard the data we gather from users of the Foosball NFT Website. Although the security of personally identifiable information is a top priority for us, we cannot ensure the security of any information you communicate to us over the Foosball NFT Website.
